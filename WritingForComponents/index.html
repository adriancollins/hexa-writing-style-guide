    Writing for components

    Follow the good guidance in the entire style guide to help you write. These guidelines are additional tips to help you create great content for components.

    Buttons

    Text on buttons must be accurate and specific. Our user should never be surprised by what happens when they press it. Don't use Yes or No. There are always exceptions but try to follow these guidelines to create buttons that really help our user.

    Start with an action verb

    Something happens when you click or tap a button. Say what happens with a verb. Navigation buttons can be an exception to this rule.

    Do not write: Replace with:
    Save Changes?
    [No] [Yes]    		 Save Changes?
    [Discard] [Save]
